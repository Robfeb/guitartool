The CAGED System is a way of mapping major chords and scales across the entire neck using five basic "open" chord shapes: C, A, G, E, and D.

  • C-Shape: Bright voicing, root on the 5th and 2nd strings.
  • A-Shape: Powerful barre chord, root on the 5th string.
  • G-Shape: Wide reach, root on the 6th, 3rd, and 1st strings.
  • E-Shape: The most common barre chord, root on 6th, 4th, and 1st strings.
  • D-Shape: High voicing, root on the 4th and 2nd strings.

As you move up the neck, these shapes connect in order: C → A → G → E → D → (repeat).

Scale patterns follow a predictable cycle. Understanding the horizontal sequence helps you break out of vertical "boxes".
